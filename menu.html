About

What is Rialo Predict?

Rialo Predict is an on-chain prediction market dApp built on the Rialo Protocol. Players build parlays — multi-pick combinations of crypto price outcomes — and earn points for correct predictions.

Points are recorded on-chain and redeemable for protocol rewards at mainnet launch. This is currently a testnet deployment — all balances are simulated.

The goal: make prediction markets fun, composable, and accessible. No finance background needed — just your conviction on where the market moves next.

Points Explained

Every correct prediction earns 1 point. Points are stored on-chain and act as your score on the protocol. When mainnet launches, accumulated points will be convertible into protocol rewards. The more picks you stack correctly in a single parlay, the bigger the multiplier on your points.
