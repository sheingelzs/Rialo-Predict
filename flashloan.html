How Flash Loans
Work

01
Request Capital

Borrow any amount from Aave's deep liquidity pool. Zero collateral. Zero credit check. All within a single block.

02
Execute Arbitrage

Buy on the cheaper DEX, simultaneously sell on the higher-priced DEX. Capture the spread delta in milliseconds.

03
Repay + Fee

Repay the original loan plus the 0.09% Aave protocol fee. If repayment fails, the entire transaction reverts atomically.

04
Net Yield

Retain the remaining profit — the spread minus protocol fees and EVM gas costs — delivered to your wallet address.
